ABOUT / 关于品牌

What BrokenLoop Is / BrokenLoop 是什么

More than a music page / 不只是一个卖歌页面

Emotion First

BrokenLoop starts from mood, memory, and unfinished feelings.

BrokenLoop 从情绪、记忆，还有那些没结束的感觉开始。

Slow Experience

This site is made to be felt slowly, not rushed through.

这个网站不是让人急着点完，而是让人慢慢沉进去。

Music as Memory

Each song is treated like a fragment of a late-night memory.

每一首歌都像一段深夜回忆的碎片被保存下来。
